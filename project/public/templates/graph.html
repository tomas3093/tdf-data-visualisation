Tour de France winners by country

Visualization of all Tour de France victories by country on interactive charts. Visualization uses data from all TdF races which took place between 1903 - 2019 (except 1915 - 1918 and 1940 - 1946). You can choose from the categories on the left and select some country column in the bar chart to view best riders from that country visualized as pie chart.
